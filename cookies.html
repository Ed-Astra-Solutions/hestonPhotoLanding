This Cookie Policy explains how Heston Photo Photography ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on hestonphoto.com. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work properly, improve performance, and provide information to site owners. We also use similar technologies such as local storage and pixels.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly necessary

These cookies are essential for the Website to function (e.g. remembering form state, security checks, session handling). They cannot be disabled.

Performance & analytics

Used to understand how visitors interact with the Website so we can improve it. These do not personally identify you.

Security (reCAPTCHA)

We use Google reCAPTCHA on our enquiry forms to protect against spam and abuse. reCAPTCHA sets cookies and processes information about your interaction with the page in line with Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Functional

Remember your preferences (e.g. closing a banner) so you have a smoother experience.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers let you:

  • See what cookies are stored and delete them individually.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from particular sites.
  • Block all cookies.

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect parts of the Website (e.g. submitting an enquiry).

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages may include embedded content (e.g. Instagram, YouTube, Google Maps). These third parties may set their own cookies, which we do not control. Please refer to the cookie policies of those providers.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
